## What Is SEC Form 10-Q?

What Is SEC Form 10-Q? 
SEC Form 10-Q is a comprehensive unaudited report of financial performance that must be submitted quarterly by all public companies to theSecurities and Exchange Commission (SEC). It stands in contrast to SEC Form 10-K, which is required to be filed annually and is audited.
